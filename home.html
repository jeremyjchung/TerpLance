Our Mission

Our goal is to create empowering websites for non-profit organizations. We believe that we can help these organizations succeed, by creating fresh modern looking websites for them. All of the websites we create are free (so long as it is for a non-profit). However, please notice the donations tab in the upper right corner... once in a while, we do get hungry.

From a time before the dinosaurs, members of Terrapin Lancers have been coding to enhance and sharpen our skills. Even the big old asteroid couldn't stop us from coding.

We ask that you give us the chance and help us help you. In other words, the Terrapin Lancers will scratch your back if you tickle ours.

That is all. Now watch us work it baby!
